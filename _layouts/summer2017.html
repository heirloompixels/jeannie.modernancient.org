Weaving has become central to my life as an artist. 6 + years ago while living in India, I became enamored, entranced and obsessed with textiles, two years later when I returned to the US, i bought a loom off of Craigslist.....and the rest is history.

After 6 years of living on the road (the last bit with the loom in the camper), we decided to plant some roots in New Mexico, the Land of Enchantment for good reason. I now fill my days weaving, sewing, dyeing, dreaming, working in the garden, marveling at the flowers, cooking good food and when ever there is a spare moment, playing in the mountains that surround our home.

I create from a deep inspiration, and I hope that my creations also inspire.
